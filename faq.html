Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own item?

In many cases, yes. Send me a photo of the item and I can let you know whether it can be engraved.

Do you create custom designs?

Absolutely. Many projects start as a simple idea, sketch, logo, or photograph.

Can you engrave photographs?

Yes. Photos can often be engraved on crystal, slate, glassware, metal cards, and other materials.

Do you travel to events?

Yes. Happy Hour Engraving is available for craft fairs, vendor markets, fundraisers, corporate events, and private gatherings.

How long do orders take?

Turnaround time depends on the project and current workload. Most orders are completed within a few days, while larger custom projects may take longer.

Do you ship?

Yes. Most products can be shipped throughout the United States.

Can I order just one item?

Absolutely. Whether you need a single personalized gift or multiple items, both are welcome.

What items can be engraved?

Coins, K9 Crystals, Coasters, Tumblers, Glassware, Business Cards, Keychains, Awards, and more.

How do I request a custom order?

Email, Instagram, or Etsy are all great ways to get in touch and discuss your project.

Still Have Questions?
