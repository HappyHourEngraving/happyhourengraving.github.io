The People and Machines Behind Happy Hour Engraving

Every engraving starts with an idea and a machine ready to bring it to life.

Meet Tom

Owner & Engraver

Hi, I'm Tom, owner of Happy Hour Engraving.

What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion for creating personalized gifts, keepsakes, memorial pieces, and custom engraved products for customers throughout New York and beyond.

Whether I'm engraving a brass challenge coin, a crystal keepsake, a custom tumbler, or working a local craft fair, every project is treated with the same attention to detail and care.

Meet The Machines

The Workshop Crew

Hugin

Named after Odin's raven. Hugin travels to craft fairs, vendor markets, and community events, bringing custom engraving directly to customers.

Rune Carver

The workhorse of the shop. Responsible for coins, tumblers, slate, business cards, keychains, and many of the custom pieces that leave the workshop.

Bifrost

Named after the rainbow bridge connecting worlds. Bifrost transforms photos and artwork into crystal keepsakes, memorials, and personalized gifts preserved within glass.
